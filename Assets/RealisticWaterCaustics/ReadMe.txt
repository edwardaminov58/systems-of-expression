Thank you for downloading Realistic Water Caustic !

The package includes several water caustic shaders.
1. Two kinds of procdeural based caustic shader.
2. Two kinds of texture based caustic shader.

Demo scene "Caustics Procedural" demonstrates the procdeural based caustic shader.
Demo scene "Caustics Textured" demonstrates the texture based caustic shader.
Open it and click play button, please take them as usage example.
